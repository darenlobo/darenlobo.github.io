About the Company

Folx, pronounced like folks, is a dating platform that matches users with others who love the same places. This makes for great dates and the foundation for great relationships.

About My Role

The folks at Folx had a basic idea about what the app needed to be, and I was called on to develop the concept, create new user flows, streamline the UI design and prototype the experience.
