Hey, I'm Daren.

I love creating elegant solutions to complex problems and crafting beautiful experiences. My growth mindset helps me see problems as challenges, and hurdles as opportunities.

Educated at the IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay, I've honed my professional skills at Fractal Ink Design Studio and Microsoft. I have also spearheaded projects for startups and liaised directly with their core teams. My diverse experience makes me a perfect fit for any work environment.

In my time off, I enjoy playing the guitar, gaming, and barbequing. I’ve also co-founded a hobbyist coding club at IDC, where we conduct tutorials & workshop sessions to help each other learn to code! (Fun fact - I built this website myself after our HTML & CSS sessions!)
